Features

Everything you need to run your travel business

Beautiful trip quotes

Build branded PDF and shareable-link proposals with day-by-day itineraries, live pricing, and multiple cover layouts. Track when clients open them.

CRM built for travel

Contacts, deals, tasks, and custom pipelines. Track every inquiry from first message to booking — with trip-specific fields operators actually use.

Your supplier catalog

Hotels, activities, destinations, transport — all in one place with rates, images, and meal plans. Pulls into every quote with one click.

AI that uses your inventory

Describe a trip in plain English and get a full itinerary drafted from your own hotels and activities. Plus AI narratives, deal summaries, and email drafts.

WhatsApp & email reminders

Assigned tasks, deal handoffs, and overdue alerts go straight to your team — via WhatsApp on higher plans, email on all.

Automations & integrations

Auto-assign leads, nudge stale deals, flag overdue tasks. Webhooks and an API key for Zapier, n8n, or your own tooling.

Pipeline analytics

Conversion rates, revenue by source, team leaderboards. See what's working and where deals are stalling — with no spreadsheets.

Your brand, everywhere

Logo, colors, fonts, and cover quotes baked into every PDF and client-facing link. White-label on Business — no "powered by" footer.

Secure client sharing

Send quotes as branded links with view tracking, optional password protection, and expiry dates. Get notified the moment they're opened or accepted.
